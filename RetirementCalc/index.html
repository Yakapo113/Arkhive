Reading this

This is a planning model, not financial advice. Everything is in today's dollars (inflation‑adjusted). Social Security and other income are treated as after‑tax; portfolio withdrawals are taxed by account type. Monte Carlo re‑runs the plan across many random return sequences to estimate how often you don't run out — real markets can do worse than any model. Verify your Social Security estimate at ssa.gov.
