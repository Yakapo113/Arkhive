Reading this

Planning model, not financial advice. Everything is in today's dollars (inflation-adjusted). Contributions stop when you retire; withdrawals are taxed by account type; RMDs are forced from 401(k)/IRA at your RMD age and reinvested if unspent. Social Security and other income are treated as after-tax. Monte Carlo re-runs the plan across many random return sequences to estimate how often you don't run out — real markets can do worse than any model. The 10% early-withdrawal penalty before 59½ isn't modeled, so set realistic "draw from" ages. Verify your Social Security estimate at ssa.gov.
